### Full description (detailed, ~450 words)
> **Turn "almost there" into bigger carts.**
>
> ShipBoost shows shoppers exactly how much more they need to spend to unlock
> free shipping — a proven nudge that increases average order value. It installs
> as a Shopify theme app block, so there's no theme code to touch and nothing to
> break. Set a goal, pick a style, choose where it appears, and it updates live
> as customers add items to their cart.
>
> **Boost average order value**
> Free shipping thresholds are one of the most reliable ways to grow cart size.
> ShipBoost makes the goal visible at the exact moment shoppers are deciding what
> to buy, encouraging them to add just one more item.
>
> **Live, real-time progress**
> The bar reads the live cart and re-renders instantly whenever the total
> changes — including a celebratory success state the moment the goal is reached.
>
> **Beautifully on brand**
> Choose from six professionally designed templates — Modern, Glass, Gradient,
> Minimal, Luxury and Neon — then fine-tune colors, border radius, bar height,
> and typography (30+ Google Fonts or your theme's own font). A live preview in
> the admin shows every change instantly.
>
> **Precise placement & targeting**
> Position the bar above or below your product information, or directly above or
> below the Add to Cart button, where it aligns perfectly with your buy button.
> Show it on the pages you choose, and toggle mobile and desktop independently.
>
> **No code. No risk. Fast.**
> ShipBoost is added and removed from your theme editor as an app block — it
> never edits your theme files. It ships a tiny script and stylesheet and only
> loads the single font you select, so your storefront stays fast.
>
> **Works with your theme**
> Compatible with Dawn, Horizon and any Online Store 2.0 theme that supports app
> blocks. Set up in minutes — no developer required.
>
> Install ShipBoost and launch your free shipping bar today.

## 4. App Icon Review

**Current mark:** a green (`#2E7D32`) rounded square containing a white upward
trend line with an arrowhead (used in the app login page and the website header).

**Assessment:** Strong and appropriate — on-brand color, communicates "growth/
boost," no text, simple enough to read at small sizes, and consistent across the
app and website. **Recommend keeping it** (no redesign needed).

**Recommendations (production polish only, not a redesign):**
1. Export a dedicated **1200 × 1200 px** PNG (Shopify's required icon size), with
   the mark at ~70–75% of the canvas so it has safe padding.
2. Verify legibility at **48 px and 16 px** (search results / browser tab). The
   trend line + arrow should stay crisp; thicken the stroke slightly if it thins
   out at small sizes.
3. Provide a solid background (the green square already does this — Shopify
   discourages transparency for app icons).
4. Keep the exact same mark on the icon, app, and website for brand recognition.
5. Optional: a very subtle inner highlight/gradient for depth — not required.
