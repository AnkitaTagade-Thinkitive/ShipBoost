Overview

A free shipping goal bar, done right

ShipBoost installs as a Shopify theme app block — no theme code to edit. Set a goal, pick a style, choose where it appears, and it updates live as customers add items to the cart.

Real-time progress

Reads the live cart and re-renders instantly whenever the total changes, including a celebratory success state at the goal.

No code, no risk

Added and removed from your theme editor as an app block. It never edits your theme files or slows your storefront down.

Fully on brand

Six templates plus custom colors, fonts, radius and messaging so the bar matches your store perfectly.
