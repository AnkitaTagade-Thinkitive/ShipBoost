This Privacy Policy explains how [COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME] (“we”, “us”) handles information in connection with the ShipBoost app (“the App”) for Shopify merchants (“you”). By installing or using the App you agree to this policy.

Information we access

The App accesses only what it needs to display a free shipping progress bar:

  • Store & account data — your Shopify store domain and an access token issued by Shopify during installation, used to authenticate API requests.
  • Theme data — read-only, to display your live theme name and status in the app dashboard (read_themes scope).
  • App settings — the configuration you enter (goal amount, colors, messages, placement, etc.), stored so your bar renders consistently.
  • Storefront cart total — read on your storefront to calculate progress toward the free shipping goal. This value is read in the shopper’s browser and is not stored by us.

Information we do not collect

ShipBoost does not collect, store, or process customer personal data (such as names, addresses, emails, or payment details). It does not use cookies to track shoppers and does not build customer profiles.

How we use information

  • To operate the App and render your progress bar on your storefront.
  • To save and apply your settings.
  • To provide support you request.
  • To maintain security and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Data sharing & sub-processors

We share data only as needed to run the service:

  • Shopify — the platform the App runs on and the source of store/theme data.
  • Hosting / infrastructure provider — [HOSTING_PROVIDER_AND_REGION], where the App and its database are hosted.

We do not share your information with any other third parties except where required by law.

Data retention

We keep your store data and settings only while the App is installed. When you uninstall the App, your session is deleted, and store data is removed in response to Shopify’s shop/redact compliance request (sent approximately 48 hours after uninstall). We also honor Shopify’s customers/redact and customers/data_request webhooks, though we hold no customer personal data.

Data security

Access tokens and settings are stored in a secured database and transmitted over HTTPS. We restrict access to authorized personnel and apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards.

Your rights

International transfers

Your data may be processed in the country where our hosting provider operates ([HOSTING_REGION]). Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.
